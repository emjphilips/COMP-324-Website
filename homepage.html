Let's Plan Your Next Trip :

First Step - Either Search the Name of the Desired City Or Pick from the Most Popular Section.

Second Step - Click the Favorite Button on an Attraction that seems interesting to you.

Third Step - Move to the Favorites Tab which will list all of the favorited attractions and a map with each one pinned, allowing you to plan your trip with ease. Finding the closest hotel and the best plan allowing you to see everything!

Fourth Step - Book a flight and a room

Fifth Step - Enjoy Your Trip!!

About TripJoy:

TripJoy, allows you to search for attractions in any city within the world which you can then favorite attractions making vacations easy to plan. Your favorited attractions will be saved so you can come back to these locations in a later date. TripJoy was created to lower the stress levels of planning vacations.



Why Pick Tripjoy:

Planning a vacation is incredibly stressful and the goal of this site is to relieve that stress. But this site complies the major attractions of each individual city. Allowing you search through each attraction and find the locations that interest you. The focus is on locations and enjoying your trip.

Other sites focus the location. Giving you one area and then having you focus on that instead of giving you that freedom.
